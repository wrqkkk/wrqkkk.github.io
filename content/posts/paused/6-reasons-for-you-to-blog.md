## Build up your personal brand

When we don't know each other, the quickest way to get familiar is to have a self-introduction like what we've been doing in our primary, junior high, senior high school and in our university, most of which serve for familiarity without other purposes.

However, when we grow up, we’re faced with all types of scenarios to introduce ourselves with a purpose, the introducer gives information, while the audience filters important information it needs out. For example, during a job interview, it's both challenging for the interviewers and the interviewees. For the interviewers, they need to figure out how to distinguish the most suitable candidate from a group of people. For the interviewees, they need to focus on how to persuade the interviewers that they are the most suitable, irreplaceable candidates.

Blogging works in this sense. When all are hard-working, when Artificial Intelligence is more hard-working than any human, what's the key difference that makes us distinguished?



## Improve writing skills


Typing word by word is becoming more and more scarce in this modern era. It seems so convenient and efficient to let AI free us from those laboring tasks where it can do way better than us.



## Stay enthusiastic

Daily checking and taking notes could remind you of what you've been doing and where you've been.
